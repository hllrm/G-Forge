**Decision (original, retained):** rebrand to **G-Proof** (Gianmarco's shipping standard for Claude Code). The G-Factor is the human in the loop; the gate enforces discipline before merge. But you can G-Proof it."** — positioning around governance layer (educated, enforced project management), not another agent orchestrator.
